# Greenroot, Series A Pitch · Notes

## What this example demonstrates

- The full **14-slide pitch spine** from `deck-pitch`, applied to a sector none of the canonical templates cover (climate tech, hardware-adjacent, B2B industrial).
- The **Warm Brand** style preset (peach + terracotta + cream, rounded Manrope sans) used in a non-consumer setting. Most warm palettes get used on lifestyle decks; this shows the preset reading correctly under a serious investor pitch.
- A **Slide 6 demo slot** built as a static screenshot of the Greenroot dispatch console (sidebar + main panel + right-rail "next actions"), with the "Powered by FluidDocs" attribution mark anchored bottom-right.
- A **competition table** built as an honest 2D matrix (capability rows, vendor columns) instead of a strawman "us vs them" comparison.
- A **bottom-up TAM** ($4.2B from 21,400 sites times $195K ACV), not a top-down "1% of a $100B market."
