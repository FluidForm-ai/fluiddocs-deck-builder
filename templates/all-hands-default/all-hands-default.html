Retro · 12 / 15
Honest reflection

Two things that didn't go to plan.

Because a slide of only wins is a slide that's hiding something.

SMB churn ticked up to 4.1%
We saw 3 percentage points of churn drift in the under-50-seat segment. Root cause: onboarding friction we knew about but hadn't prioritized. The bulk import release helps, but not enough on its own.
What we'll do New onboarding flow is priority #3 above. Owner is shared between Product and CS, weekly cadence starting June 3.
EMEA launch delayed to Q3
We told customers May. We're now looking at August at the earliest. Data residency requirements were heavier than we modeled, and we don't want to ship half-ready.
What we'll do Direct email to the 47 EMEA prospects this week. New date locked, no slippage past August 31. Updates in #launch-emea.
